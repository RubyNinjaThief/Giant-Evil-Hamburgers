How I got it

I went to my local card store, and when I saw the neat-looking dude on the box with the flashy eyes, I thought, Whoa, cool! and I impusively bought it. The cards are pretty powerful, and as implied on the box, it happens to be built upon the mechanic of pendulum summoning.

Kaiba Tin

Where did I get it??

My local department store toy section. I must have stood there for about 10 minutes deciding between getting a box of packs or a tin. I finally made the choice to get the tin, since it's a way to store my plethora of cards, and comes with rares.
